## About Me

I'm a co-founder of **Raisi**. I build and ship things people actually need, with a strong focus on **product and growth engineering**.

I started building when I was thirteen and launched my first company at eighteen. We reached **$200k ARR**, then lost the business overnight due to geopolitics.

After that, I moved to the US and worked on growth across multiple startups. Eventually, I founded my own products:
- An **AI unified workspace for founders** to eliminate time drains (email, tasks, scheduling)
- A **B2B ABM AI platform** for early-stage startups
- And finally **Raisi**, where everything clicked and became serious
